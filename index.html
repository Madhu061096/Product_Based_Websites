About Us

Team Phrotons is dedicated to harnessing clean energy from the most abundant element in the universe; Hydrogen. We have developed our in-house Hydrogen Fuel Cell and other supporting technologies like power management, thermal management systems and composite storage. With the vision of supporting the Sustainable Development Goals, and having research and development at our core, we look forward to building professional relationships within and beyond the automotive industry.


Technology

Fuel Cell

The most promising emerging green energy source. Hydrogen Fuel Cell generates electricity through an electrochemical reaction between stored hydrogen, and the atmospheric oxygen, where the only byproduct is clean water.


Power Control

With great powers comes great responsibilities. Our specifically designed Power Control unit manages the entire energy generation and storage unit.

Thermal Management

Ok cool down. Thermal management system developed at Phrotons Mobility ensures adequate cooling of the entire system. It can also be used independently in other electronic cooling applications.

Hydrogen Storage

Store more is at our core. Our storage system reduces the storage volume of hydrogen by around 20 times. This serves in developing a compact assembly and hence a lighter system.

Green Hydrogen

Can we ever have enough green? We produce green Hydrogen as part of the National Hydrogen Mission emphasised by PM Modi. This hydrogen fuels the cleanest energy generation systems.
