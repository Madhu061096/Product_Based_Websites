Technology

Fuel Cell

Hydrogen Fuel Cell generates electricity through an electrochemical reaction between stored hydrogen, and the atmospheric oxygen. The only byproduct here is clean water. Starting with heavy vehicles, Hydrogen Fuel Cell is all set to take over the majority transportation industry in the years to come. Major automobile manufacturers have openly supported this technology and vowed to switch to an all-HFC powered mobility very soon.

Power Control

Our specifically designed Power Control unit manages the entire energy generation and storage unit.

Thermal Management

Thermal management system developed at Phrotons Mobility ensures adequate cooling of the entire system. It can also be used independently in other electronic cooling applications.

Hydrogen Storage

Our storage system reduces the storage volume of hydrogen by around 20 times. This serves in developing a compact assembly and hence a lighter system.

Green Hydrogen

We produce Green Hydrogen as part of the National Hydrogen Mission emphasised by PM Modi. This hydrogen fuels the cleanest energy generation systems. Hydrogen gas is generated by electrolysis of water. This electrolysis, when done using renewable energy sources, generates Green Hydrogen separating the hydrogen atom within it from its molecular twin oxygen. In simple words, our technology supports zero carbon footprints from start to finish of the fuel lifecycle. Honorable Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has only strengthened the Hydrogen Power industry by promoting it in his 75th Independence Day press release. As proposed in the budget 2021, FInance Minister Smt. Nirmala Sitharaman has announced a budget of 1500 Cr for the National Hydrogen Energy Mission..
