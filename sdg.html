Sustainable Development Goal

The Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), also known as the Global Goals, were adopted by the United Nations in 2015 as a universal call to action to end poverty, protect the planet, and ensure that by 2030 all people enjoy peace and prosperity.

We, at Phrotons Mobility, are a significant ally to these goals. We are determined to achieve the following goals as a direct result of the nature of our business. We also do promote and assist other companies work for these goals by making the green energy solution affordable.

SDG 7
Ensure access to affordable, reliable, sustainable and modern energy for all

SDG 9

Build resilient infrastructure, promote inclusive and sustainable industrialization and foster innovation

SDG 12

Ensure sustainable consumption and production patterns

SDG 13

Take urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts
